About the Role

This is an exciting time to join Lightfoots as we grow our Employment Law offering within the firm led by our Head of Employment Law. This role will support the growth, build new relationships and maintain our existing client relationships advising on all Employment Law matters to an exceptional standard. The ideal candidate will have a passion for Employment Law and keeping up to date on legislation.

Key Responsibilities

  • Drafting and checking documentation for our clients in accordance with Employment Legislation
  • Providing answers to incoming queries from clients in a precise and timely manner
  • Highlighting any potential risk to the clients in order to protect their business
  • Recognise areas within policies, procedures or contracts that need updating and expanding
  • Ensure that all advice given to clients is recorded/documented appropriately
  • Handle Employment Tribunal Matters efficiently
  • Advise clients on all Employment related matters
  • Work with colleagues and the Head of Employment to share knowledge and provide support

Skills & Experience

  • 1-2 years PQE
  • Previous experience in a similar role
  • Excellent communication and influencing skills
  • Ability to prioritise workload
  • Up to date on all employment legislation
